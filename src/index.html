About Me

Hello, and welcome. My name is Richard Lovelace and this is my coding journey...

I’ve spent most of my career working in the restaurant industry. I took a summer job as a busboy in a local restaurant, and I was hooked. The organized chaos of a restaurant was fascinating, and I wanted to know how it all worked. I learned a lot, and I loved what I did. As a result, I became an exceptional server, and line cook. After a great career, working in some really wonderful places, I began to feel as if I still had more to accomplish. I wanted to use my mind to make a living, instead of my feet.

After considerable thought, and some extensive research, I began to see the writing on the wall. I realized that programming offered everything that I was looking for. I started taking some courses at San Antonio College, when I heard about Lambda School. A fully immersive, nine-month long coding school, where I learned full-stack development. At eight hours a day, five days a week, it was an extreme challenge. I struggled a lot, but it was the best decision I ever made regarding my career. Lambda taught me something infinitely more valuable than coding skills. They taught me how to learn.

Since completing Lambda’s Web Development curriculum, things have been going quite well. I was recently commissioned to design, and build a website for a local business, which can be seen above. It was a rewarding experience, and only strengthened my resolve to become a professional web developer. I have discovered a passion for coding, particularly UI, that well surpasses anything I felt for the restaurant industry. I am currently working on a e-commerce app using React Hooks and Context, as well as a vanilla JavaScript app, as yet to be determined. I intend on excelling in my newly chosen field, and I hope that this portfolio conveys the devotion I have to the craft, and my desire to become a full-time developer with your company.
